# Mindwell (Mental Health Website)


## Description
I have a created a mental health website as a creative project, which is mainly targeting female audience as it's consist of mood tracker where she reviews the mood on day to day base like how she is feeling and adding note on it to specify it, then I have a stress management system which is consist of breathing exercise which contain timers from 5 mins upto 10 mins as per your conviences, i have also integrated some soothing nature sounds to help with stree and anxiety and daily journaling to let go off the thoughts, then I have added a assessment which determines how bad the anxiety/depression is hitting them and further they consult to a medical expert. Additionally I have provided a chatbot for answering through questions related to mental health.
